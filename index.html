⚙️ Step-by-Step Safe Apply Process

  • 1
    Passport check

    Passport me kam se kam 2 saal ki validity honi chahiye, name aur signature sahi ho.

  • 2
    Source checked information only

    Kisi bhi broker se visa na lein. Sathi AI par direct licensed recruiting companies ya direct job roadmaps se guide lein.

  • 3
    Written labor contract verification

    Sponsorship, basic salary, rehna, khana, air ticket, aur duty hours contract mein check karein.

  • 4
    PCC & Medical reports

    Hamesha govt approved center se medical karein aur clean Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) upload karein.

✓ Required Documents Checklist

  • ✓ Passport front & back scan (high clear quality)
  • ✓ Experience Certificate/Vocational training card
  • ✓ GAMCA / Approved Medical clearance copy
  • ✓ Police Clearance certificate (PCC)
  • ✓ Labor Contract signed copy

Visa Fraud Verification Check

⚠️ ALWAYS AVOID VISIT/TOURIST VISA FOR JOBS

Visit/tourist visa par naukri karna illegal hai. Agent log visit visa par bhej kar work visa lagwane ka vaada karte hain, jo 99% fake hota hai. Jail, deport aur ban ho sakte hain.

🔍 Real vs Fake Visa Checkpoints

Government Portal QR Check

Wazarat / Labour Ministry website par passport se verify karein.

License Check of Recruiter

Recruitment agency ka MEA / Government license check karein.

Zero Cash Without Receipt

Paisa dekar agar official legal written receipt nahi mili toh fraud 100% taye hai.

Money & Govt Help Guide

🛡️ IMPORTANT SAFE REMITTANCE & WELFARE STATEMENT

BaharKaam is not a bank, remittance vendor, money transfer outlet, nor do we issue micro-loans. Hum direct payments or loan disbursements nahi karte. Hum pravasi parivaro ko financial fraud se bachane ke liye standard sarkari yojanaon aur surakshit banking channels ka guide dete hain.

🏛️ Official Govt Schemes & Welfare Loans

Videsh jaane ke liye brokers se high-interest interest loans na lein. Sarkari banks aur welfare funds se contact karein:

🇮🇳 India Welfare & Loans

eMigrate Portal registration, SBI Pravasi Loan, and PBBY (Pravasi Bharatiya Bima Yojana) 10 Lakh insurance safety wrapper.

🛡️ Safe Remittance Protection Rules

  • 1
    Never use Hawala / Hundi

    Private offline hawala agents illegal hain. Hawala se bheja paisa freeze ho sakta hai aur aapka account block ho sakta hai.

  • 2
    Safe receipts custody

    Har digital ya physical receipt ko secure PDF bana kar WhatsApp par family ke sath save rakhein.

  • 3
    Debt and loan awareness

    Ghar se local broker se high-interest loan le kar videsh jana sabse bada trap hai. Sarkaari welfare migration loan hi check karein.
