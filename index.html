Visa & Ticket Fraud Verification

⚠️ ALWAYS AVOID VISIT/TOURIST VISA FOR JOBS

Visit/tourist visa par naukri karna illegal hai. Agent log visit visa par bhej kar work visa lagwane ka vaada karte hain, jo 99% fake hota hai. Jail, deport aur ban ho sakte hain.

🔍 Real vs Fake Visa Checkpoints

Government Portal QR Check

Wazarat / Labour Ministry website par passport se verify karein.

License Check of Recruiter

Recruitment agency ka MEA / Government license check karein.

Zero Cash Without Receipt

Paisa dekar agar official legal written receipt nahi mili toh fraud 100% taye hai.

Money & Govt Help Guide

🛡️ IMPORTANT SAFE REMITTANCE & WELFARE STATEMENT

BaharKaam is not a bank, remittance vendor, money transfer outlet, nor do we issue micro-loans. Hum direct payments or loan disbursements nahi karte. Hum pravasi parivaro ko financial fraud se bachane ke liye standard sarkari yojanaon aur surakshit banking channels ka guide dete hain.

💰 Total Abroad Cost Estimate

Videsh jaane ke liye brokers bohot zyada paise demand karte hain. Asli sarkaari aur standard fees bohot kam hoti hain:

📋 Work Visa Fee (Official Govt): ₹10,000 - ₹25,000
✈️ One-way Flight Ticket: ₹15,000 - ₹30,000
🏥 GAMCA Medical + Insurance: ₹5,000 - ₹8,000
Total Realistic Cost: ₹30,000 - ₹63,000 approx.

*Agar koi agent aapse ₹2-3 Lakh se zyada maang raha hai, toh woh extra commission le raha hai ya fake visa de raha hai. Be careful!

🛡️ Agent Payment Safety & Receipt Rules

Apni mehnat ki kamai ko surakshit rakhne ke liye in rules ka palan karein:

  • Zero Advance Cash: Visa haath mein aane se pehle aur verification se pehle koi bhi bada amount advance na deinn.
  • Always Take Stamped Receipts: Agent ko koi bhi cash ya online payment dene par uski signed aur stamped receipt zaroori lein. Receipt safety sabse important hai!
  • Send Photo to Family: Receipt milte hi uski clear photo khinchkar turant apne parivaar ke sath WhatsApp par save karein.
  • Official Source Verification Reminder: Kisi bhi agent ki credibility check karne ke liye sarkar ke eMigrate (India) ya BE&OE (Pakistan) portal par unka license ID zaroor verify karein.

⚠️ Loan / Byaaj Warning: Avoid Loan Sharks

Local brokers ya village loan sharks (baniya/byaajkhor) se 5% - 10% monthly interest par loan lena sabse bada trap hai. Videsh jaakar aapki aadhi salary sirf byaaj chukane mein chali jayegi.

Better Alternative: Apne documents ke sath sarkaari aur registered cooperative banks mein jaakar low-interest migration/personal loans ke bare mein poochein. Sarkaari welfare migration loans saste hote hain.

🛡️ Safe Money Transfer & No Hawala Warning

Videsh se ghar paise bhejne ke liye HUNDI / HAWALA ka upyog bilkul na karein. Yeh gair-kanooni hai aur:

  • Hawala se bheja paisa freeze ho sakta hai aur aapke account par police enquiry aa sakti hai.
  • Hamesha official mobile banking apps, direct bank wires, ya verified exchanges ka hi upyog karein.
  • Ghar par parivaar ko bolen ki paise aate hi bank se confirmation check karein, kisi anjaan phone call par vishwas na karein.
